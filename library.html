  • Access to the library is restricted to students and faculty members of Gnana Vikas Polytechnic.
  • Identity card is must for library services
  • Bags and personal things not allowed beyond the issue counter
  • Use of mobile Phone is strictly prohibited
  • Photography/Video recording is not allowed
  • Keep the books in proper place

Library and information center


The college library is located in separate block and has a large collection of books pertaining to diploma engineering, management, applied sciences, applied maths and topics of general interest. The collection is continuously added to cater to the needs of students and teachers. The primary Mission of the library is to support the educational programs of the institute by providing the physical and intellectual access to information.
